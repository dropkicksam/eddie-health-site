A real health assessment built for the moment you became a dad — not a quiz, not a wellness app. A proper checkup that fits into your life.

What to expect

01

Answer a few questions

Before anything else, you'll complete a short intake covering your health history, lifestyle, sleep, stress, and what's changed since you became a dad. It takes about 10 minutes and sets the agenda for everything that follows.

~10 minutes Done on your schedule No office visit needed
02

Meet with a provider

A licensed clinician reviews your intake and meets with you virtually — on your time, not theirs. You'll talk through what's on your mind, what's changed in your body, and what you actually want to work on. No rushed waiting room, no awkward small talk.

Virtual visit Licensed clinician Evening & weekend slots
03

Get your labs done

Based on your visit, your provider orders targeted bloodwork — not a generic panel, but tests that make sense for you. You go to a local lab at a time that works, and your results come back to your provider for review.

Targeted bloodwork Local lab network Results reviewed by your provider
04

Get your plan

Once your labs are back, your provider puts together a personalized health summary — what's going well, what needs attention, and a clear set of next steps. Whether that's a treatment plan, a specialist referral, or just some peace of mind, you'll walk away knowing where you stand.

Personalized summary Next steps & referrals Ongoing support available
